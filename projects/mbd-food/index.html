Our Story

Built on taste.

What started as a small dhaba in 2015 is now a favorite spot for food lovers.

MBD

MBD Food began with a simple belief — good food brings people together. For over 10 years, we've been serving authentic North Indian, Mughlai, and Chinese cuisine made from fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.

Every dish is prepared to order. Our tandoor is lit fresh each morning, our spices are ground in-house, and our curries are slow-cooked the way they should be. No shortcuts, no compromises — just honest food that tastes like home.

— MBD Food Team
