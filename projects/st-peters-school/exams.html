Exam Guidelines

Important instructions for students appearing in examinations.

Reporting Time

Report 15 minutes before the exam starts. Late entries not allowed after 10 minutes.

What to Bring

Admit card, stationery (pen, pencil, ruler, eraser). Electronic devices strictly prohibited.

Exam Code

Follow school uniform code. Maintain discipline. Malpractice leads to disqualification.
