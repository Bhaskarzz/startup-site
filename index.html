01

St. Peter's International School

A modern, parent-friendly school website that makes admissions easy.

The challenge: Most school websites in India are outdated — cluttered navigation, no mobile support, and critical info buried three clicks deep. Parents struggle to find admission details, fee structures, and contact information.

What we built: A clean, fast-loading school portal with an intuitive admissions journey. Hero section with school branding and virtual tour teaser. Clear pathways for prospective parents (admissions, academics, campus life) and current parents (events calendar, WhatsApp updates). Integrated inquiry form that routes directly to the admissions office via WhatsApp.

Key improvements: Mobile-first responsive layout, streamlined navigation with mega-menu for academics/programs, embedded Google Maps with nearby transport routes, photo gallery with campus facilities, and a sticky "Enquire Now" button visible on every page. Load time reduced by 60% compared to typical school sites.
Restaurant Mobile-First Menu
02

MBD Food

A restaurant website that turns visitors into diners.

The challenge: Restaurant websites in India still use PDF menus, have no mobile ordering, and miss the Google Maps + review integration that diners expect. Visitors land, get frustrated, and leave — straight to Zomato or Swiggy.

What we built: A mouth-watering, mobile-first restaurant site built around the menu. Full HTML menu with category filters (veg/non-veg, cuisine type), food photography placeholders, and one-tap WhatsApp ordering. Google Maps embed with directions, live Google Reviews integration, and a sticky "Order Now" button that follows the user.

Key improvements: No PDF menus — fully searchable HTML menu with dietary tags. Click-to-call phone number always visible. Table booking enquiry via WhatsApp. Location + hours in the first 200px of every page. Load time optimized for 3G connections — critical for Indian users. Estimated conversion improvement of 25-40% over a typical restaurant site.
