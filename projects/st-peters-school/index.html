About Us

A school with a soul.

For over 25 years, St. Peter's has been nurturing young minds with a perfect blend of academics, sports, and values.

At St. Peter's International School, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. Our mission is to nurture each child's intellectual, emotional, and social development in a safe, supportive environment.

With a student-teacher ratio of 25:1, smart classrooms, and a 10-acre green campus, we provide everything your child needs to thrive. Our CBSE curriculum is complemented by co-curricular activities, sports, and life skills training.
