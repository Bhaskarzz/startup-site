About

Our story.

Two friends, one mission: get every Indian small business online.

India has over 60 million small businesses — tiffin services, salons, tutors, bakeries, photographers. But most of them don't have a website. They rely on WhatsApp and word-of-mouth, missing out on customers who search online.

We started Startup to change that. We build affordable, professional templates that any business owner can use. No coding. No expensive agencies. Just a clean website in minutes.
